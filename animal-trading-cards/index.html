Gray tabbies flaunt stunning silver-gray patterns, always marked by that mysterious "M" on their brows.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: About 46 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 12-16 years
  • Habitat: Domestic (worldwide)

Tabby means pattern, not breed. Gray tabbies appear in many breeds with swirled, striped, spotted, or ticked markings. Playful, curious, and affectionate, they thrive in any home.
